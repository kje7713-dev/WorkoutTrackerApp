# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 20, 2024**

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Savage By Design ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information Collection

### Information We Collect

- **Workout Data**: The App stores workout blocks, exercises, sessions, and training history locally on your device.
- **App Usage**: Basic usage statistics may be collected to improve app functionality.
- **Subscription Information**: When you subscribe, Apple processes the transaction and provides us with subscription status information.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, address)
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts or photos
- We do NOT share your workout data with third parties
- We do NOT use analytics or advertising trackers

## How We Use Your Information

- **Local Storage**: All workout data is stored locally on your device
- **Subscription Management**: We use subscription status to determine feature access
- **App Improvement**: Anonymous usage data may be used to improve app functionality

## Data Storage

- All workout data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
- No workout data is transmitted to external servers
- You can delete all data at any time through the Data Management feature

## Third-Party Services

### Apple In-App Purchases

We use Apple's In-App Purchase system for subscription management. Apple processes all payment information. We do not have access to your payment details.

### External Content

The App may allow you to import workout plans from external sources (such as AI assistants). This content is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data at any time
- Cancel your subscription at any time
- Request information about data we collect

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Subscription-Specific Privacy

### What the Subscription Unlocks

The subscription unlocks in-app software features including:

#### Currently Implemented
- AI-assisted plan ingestion and parsing (import JSON from external AI tools)
- JSON workout import functionality
- AI prompt templates for external use
- Block library management for AI-generated content

#### Planned Features
- Automated review and modification tools
- Intelligent progress analysis
- Advanced dashboards and history views

### What the Subscription Does NOT Include

- The subscription does NOT provide AI or LLM access
- The subscription does NOT include external content generation
- The subscription does NOT transmit your data to external servers

### Data Processing

- Subscription status is checked locally using Apple's StoreKit framework
- No personal data is transmitted for subscription verification beyond what Apple requires
- Subscription status is cached locally on your device

## Apple Required Disclosures

This app uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment processing: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
