# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [Date]**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WorkoutTrackerApp ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

WorkoutTrackerApp stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Workout Data**: Training blocks, exercise templates, workout sessions, sets, reps, weights, and performance metrics
- **Exercise Library**: Custom exercises you create or modify
- **Training History**: Historical workout data and completion records

### Information We Don't Collect

We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Photos or media
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or usage data
- Any data outside of workout tracking

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for:
- Displaying your workout history
- Tracking your training progress
- Providing workout recommendations
- Calculating progressive overload

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

- All data is stored **exclusively on your device** in the app's sandboxed storage
- Data is protected by iOS device encryption
- We do not have access to your data
- No data is transmitted to external servers

### Data Backup

- Data can be backed up through iOS device backups (iCloud or iTunes)
- Backup behavior follows iOS system settings
- We do not control or have access to your iOS backups

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit your data over the internet
- Have access to your workout data

## Third-Party Services

### Analytics

Currently, WorkoutTrackerApp does **NOT** use any analytics services.

If we add analytics in the future:
- We will update this policy
- We will only use privacy-focused analytics
- You will have the option to opt out

### Crash Reporting

Currently, WorkoutTrackerApp does **NOT** use crash reporting services.

If we add crash reporting in the future:
- We will update this policy
- Only technical crash data will be collected (no personal information)
- You will be notified before implementation

## Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- Data is stored in iOS sandboxed directories
- iOS device encryption protects your data
- No network transmission of workout data
- No server-side storage
- Regular security updates through App Store

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Delete**: Delete any workout data through the app interface
- **Export**: Export your data (if available in app features)
- **Uninstall**: Complete data removal by deleting the app

## Children's Privacy

WorkoutTrackerApp is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us.

## International Users

Your data is stored locally on your device, regardless of your location. No data is transferred internationally.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted:
- In the app
- On our website
- In App Store update notes (for significant changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect personal information, these rights are automatically satisfied.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over these rights.
