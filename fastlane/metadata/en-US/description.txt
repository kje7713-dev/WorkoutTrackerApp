Track your workouts with precision using structured block periodization.

Killin' It is the ultimate workout tracking app for athletes, coaches, and fitness enthusiasts who follow structured training programs. Built for strength training and conditioning workouts, this app helps you plan, execute, and track your fitness journey.

KEY FEATURES:

• Training Block Management
Create multi-week training blocks with progressive overload built in. Design your program once and let the app handle the progression.

• Comprehensive Exercise Library
Access a wide range of exercises organized by category (squat, hinge, press, pull, conditioning, and more). Add your own custom exercises anytime.

• Real-Time Workout Tracking
Log your workouts as you go with an intuitive interface. Track sets, reps, weight for strength training, and time, distance, or calories for conditioning.

• Advanced Training Structures
Support for supersets, circuits, and complex training methodologies. Build workouts that match your programming style.

• Block History
Review your completed workouts and track your progress over time. See how you've improved week over week.

• AI-Assisted Programming
Get started quickly with AI-generated training blocks based on your goals and experience level.

• Progressive Overload
Automatically apply weekly progression to your training with customizable rules for weight, volume, or custom parameters.
